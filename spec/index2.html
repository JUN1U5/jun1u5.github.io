Advent Series

Holy Longing in Turbulent Times

During Advent, we will be looking at readings often overlooked in the lectionary: the psalms and songs of Scripture. Written over hundreds of years -- over times as prosperity as well as times of destruction, displacement, and exile -- these texts express Israel's longing for justice, hope, and redemption in a troubled and unpredictable world.

What do these songs of hope and longing have to teach us in these turbulent times? On Sunday mornings in Advent, join the clergy of St. Paul's as we explore these texts and discern how Christ's redemption is being made manifest among us, even today.
